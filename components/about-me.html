About me

Who am I?

My name is Lesley Hutchinson, and I am 19 years old. I started as a Java Developer in 2017 and on December 2, 2018, I made my first Java plug-in public on spigotmc.org. I got a lot of positive feedback from the people who downloaded the plugins. Not only that, but I made 8 plugins public in total and got over 5000 downloads. In 2019, I started Mobile Development with the Dart and Swift programming languages, then I made the Apple App Break and Surprise public on the Apple App Store. In 2020, I started Web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery. I found my place in this, and to this day I enjoy making websites.
